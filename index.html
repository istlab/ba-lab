Processing and visualization of open data

These are the student projects on processing and visualization of open data made in the Big Data Systems course, which is part of the MSc in Business Analytics postgraduate degree, offerred by the Department of Management Science and Technology at the Athens University of Economics and Business.

Course professor: Diomidis Spinellis
Teaching assistant: Marios Fragkoulis

Class of 2015

  • Interactive charts that show metrics of the greek economy for the period 2001 to 2014 — Ioannis Kyriakos and Ioannis Anastasios Skoufos
  • Which greek museums attracted the most vistors in 2014? — Chatzimoschou Angeliki and Karabatsos Stasinos
  • Fishery volume across the greek territory between 2002 and 2006 — Vassilis Tsakalos and Ioannis Georgopoulos
  • Stock market values of Alpha bank and Eurobank between 2007 and 2014 — Valantis Georgopoulos and Mantzanas Dimitrios
  • Car accidents in Greece between 2010 and 2011 — Martha Plexida and Benardou Panoraia
  • Mean population across greek territory in 2010 and 2011 — Aris Tsianakas and Charalambos Tsourakis
  • Unemployment in Greece between 2005 and 2014 — Ioannis Kangas and Dimitris Kotsiras
  • What kind of greek products do consumers of a certain age buy? — Ioanna Mantzouratou and Georgia Papaioannou
  • Do Greeks stay in Greece for vacations? (2006-07 data) — Eleni Kapodistria and Evangelos Kaslis
